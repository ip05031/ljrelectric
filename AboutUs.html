LJR Electric

At LJR Electric we understand the importance of having reliable and safe electrical systems in your home and business. Our team of highly trained and certified professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional electrical installation, maintenance, and repair services tailored to your specific requirements. For our residential customers, we offer a wide range of services, from installing complete electrical systems in new construction to upgrading and modernizing existing installations. Our goal is to ensure that your home is equipped with safe, efficient, and state-of-the-art electrical solutions, comfort, and peace of mind for you and your family.

For our commercial customers, we understand the importance of keeping your electrical installations running smoothly and efficiently. Whether you need a new installation, regular maintenance, or an emergency solution, our team of experts will be there for you. We work closely with you to understand your business needs and design custom solutions that optimize energy performance and reduce operating costs.

Mision

Our mission is to provide innovative and sustainable energy solutions to our customers, contributing to the economic and social development of the communities we serve. We commit to: Supply reliable electricity, drive sustainability and Constantly innovate.

Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable and reliable electric power in the United States, driving the transition to a cleaner energy future and promoting energy efficiency in all sectors.
